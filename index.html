    Take control of your digital identity with Slide.

Open a fully-functioning credit account in seconds. 

Slide enables you to transfer any of your information—from proof of who you are to your credit score—in seconds. Once the transfer is confirmed, your new account details and usage statistics will be added to your profile automatically. Use Slide to connect with services from loyalty to finance.

Check into a hospital without glancing at a form.

Slide can consolidate your patient records, personal information, and health insurance as you walk through the door. Our interfaces connect all of your providers to your profile so that your information is up-to-date and easily shareable. The same model is effective from healthcare to e-commerce.

Enroll your children in school as you walk them to the gate. 

There's no need to use your personal computer to access Slide's tools. Our mobile app makes it possible to transfer your family's important information no matter where you are, on all common interfaces. Better yet, there's no need to actively keep up to date with changes in important information: grades, emergency contacts, permission slips, and so on. Slide does it all for you.

Slide allows you to take control of your digital identity.

From establishing relationships without forms to keeping all of your personal details up-to-date, Slide functions as an easily shareable and dynamic digital identity.

Create your Slide identity.

Become a user by entering your mobile number into one of our interfaces and choosing a master password. This can be done by downloading the app, completing any Slide form for the first time, or by redeeming pre-existing data profiles with banks, loyalty programs, universities, and so on.


Once your profile is established we will help you import the rest of your data from any password manager or autofill platform you currently use.

Form relationships. 

You can initiate Slide transfers by responding to geofenced requests, for example as you walk into a bank; by searching for nearby organizations, perhaps at a student activities fair; or by browsing through Slide’s catalog of partner companies. In each case you will be able to review the data request, add or modify any responses, and choose whether or not to complete the transfer.


If you choose to establish the relationship any new responses will be added to your Slide profile for future use, and the organization will be able to send important information back to you through Slide's relationships tab. This information normally includes a combination of membership numbers, transfer history, account details, special promotions, and additional data requests. 

Stay connected.

If any of the information you have previously sent to partner companies changes or if you add new data to your profile, Slide will automatically update the companies you care about. Similarly, if data they have about you — perhaps you are issued a new credit card or earn additional miles through a frequent flier program—those updates will be reflected in your profile.


Every piece of data added to your wallet is cryptographically linked to the individual or organization that set it. This makes it possible to easily prove your identity or information when entering into new relationships. Use your Slide profile to walk into a bar or collect your prescription.

Never repeat yourself. 

Once you have responded to a given question—for example, relating to your shoe size or medical aid details—you will never have to re-enter the same information again. Instead, when responding to data requests, you will have access to all previous responses, making the process of creating and maintaining relationships rapid and painless. 


All of this information is split between two profiles: your global profile, which contains your most commonly needed information, and local profiles related to different industries and partner organizations. This makes it easy to manage all of your data intuitively through one system. 

Your identity is protected by cutting-edge encryption technology.

Hacking into your Slide profile would take every computer on earth more time than the universe has been in existence. Read more here.

Nothing happens without your explicit authorization.

Every time an organization requests copies of your information, they must ask your permission. You can choose to accept or reject any of these requests.

Slide Identity Inc. is always blind to your data. All we know is your mobile number.

Slide's system is architected so that not even our top engineering executives can access your data without your permission. And if we can't read it, no one else can.
